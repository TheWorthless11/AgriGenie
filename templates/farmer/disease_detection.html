Tips for Best Results
  1. Good Lighting: Take photos in good natural light
  2. Close-up Shots: Get as close as possible to affected areas
  3. Multiple Angles: Take photos from different angles
  4. Focus on Affected Parts: Show symptoms clearly
  5. High Resolution: Use high-quality camera/phone
  6. Clear Images: Avoid blurry or dark photos
  7. Full Leaf/Plant: Include some healthy parts for comparison
