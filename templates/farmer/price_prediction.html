How Price Prediction Works

Our AI-powered system analyzes market trends, seasonal patterns, and historical data to predict crop prices for the next 30 days. The predictions help farmers make informed decisions about the best time to harvest and sell their crops.

  • Trend Analysis: Identifies overall market direction (increasing/decreasing)
  • Volatility Assessment: Measures price fluctuation and market uncertainty
  • Best Sell Date: Recommends optimal time to maximize profit
  • Price Range: Shows minimum and maximum expected prices
